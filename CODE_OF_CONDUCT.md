# Code of Conduct

[English](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

## Our Goal

We want this project to be a collaborative, respectful, and technically constructive space.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and assume good intent
- give feedback that is specific, honest, and helpful
- welcome different levels of experience
- keep discussions focused on the work and the user impact
- protect private information and avoid sharing secrets

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following is not acceptable:

- harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- deliberately disruptive behavior in issues, reviews, or discussions
- hostile gatekeeping or repeated bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent removal from project spaces.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainer through a private channel when possible. If a private channel is not available yet, open a minimal issue requesting a confidential contact path without posting sensitive details publicly.
